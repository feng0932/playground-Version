## Account And Privacy Red Lines

涉及账户、登录、注册、手机号、验证码、第三方登录、支付、内容发布、AIGC、营销数据或个人信息处理时，先完成产品合规前置判断：

- 业务类型：tool / AIGC / content / social / enterprise service / other
- 平台与市场：iOS / Android / HarmonyOS / Web / mainland China / overseas
- 登录必要性：guest-accessible features、login-required features、strong-verification or strong-identity features、强制首屏登录理由
- 第三方能力：SMS verification、one-click login、WeChat / Apple / other open platforms、external risk-control or push services
- 特殊主题：minors、complaints / appeals / customer service、withdrawal of consent、data retention、access / correction / deletion / export / account deletion rights
